Small Animal Facility

IISER Kolkata's Small Animal Facility (SAF) is registered by the "Committee for the Purpose of Control and Supervision of Experiments on Animals (CPCSEA)”, a body of the Government of India. The “Institutional Animal Ethics Committee (IAEC)" evaluates and approves all the conduct of animal experiments as per CPCSEA rules & regulations. The Animal facility, facilitates animal research of immune-competence and suppressive small animals under a well established "Animal Bio-Safety Level 2 (ABSL2)" state of the art facility. The facility is well equipped with modern instruments as per scientific standards, and houses a well trained technical staff & various scientific teams.
